About Gene Carpenter

Hello! My name is Gene Carpenter and I am a licensed Massage Therapist with more than 20 years of experience. I also have additional training in CranioSacral Therapy from the Upledger Institute, Esalen Massage, Medical Massage from David Morin with Health Matters Seminars, Sports Massage, and I continuously add to my knowledge base. I utilize many different modalities and my own injury experience to give you a customized, healing massage. While a nice soothing massage is great, I love the challenge of alleviating problems that a client presents.

I am a very active cyclist, hiker, snowboarder, rock climber, camper, and backpacker. I've also studied dance and martial arts. Along with all this and the experience of being in a life-threatening accident with major injuries, I feel like I am able to intuitively find your problem areas and customize the massage to you. I enjoy helping people feel better and out of pain the way I was once helped (and continue to be) with mine.
