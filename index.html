Massage Services

Body Elemental specializes in creating a customized massage that serves your needs using the following modalities:

Swedish

Swedish Massage

Swedish massage involves the use of kneading, stroking and tapping by the practitioner to increase the oxygen flow in the blood and release toxins from the muscles. Swedish massage techniques can make your body feel relaxed and energized at the same time by kneading tense muscles and improving circulation.

Deep Tissue

Deep Tissue

Deep tissue massage is ideal for breaking up scar tissue and breaking down muscle knots that can cause pain and inflammation. If you suffer from chronic aches and pains, deep tissue massage can help. The practitioner may use fingertips, knuckles, hands and elbows to release tension or realign tissue fibers, and you may be asked to breathe deeply as he or she works on particularly tense areas. It is normal to feel some stiffness or soreness after a deep tissue massage, but this usually subsides within 24 hours.

Sports

Sports Massage

Sports Massage focuses on injuries and imbalances that accompany the repetitive motion of a sports-related or work-related activity. The practitioner uses techniques similar to Swedish massage to speed up the healing process of muscle pulls and strains, or to keep these regularly- worked muscles from tensing up and causing injuries.

Medical

Medical Massage

Unlike a traditional spa massage focused on relaxation, medical massage therapy is a specialized, outcome-based treatment designed to address specific health concerns and promote lasting relief and healing. Body Elemental will work with you to develop a personalized treatment plan incorporating techniques like deep tissue massage, trigger point therapy, and myofascial release, all aimed at reducing pain, inflammation, and improving your overall well-being. If you're seeking a targeted approach to managing chronic pain or recovering from an injury, consider exploring the transformative benefits of medical massage therapy.

Esalen

Esalen

Esalen massage is known for its long strokes that target the muscles and circulatory system combined with the classical techniques of Swedish massage. The primary focus of Esalen massage is on touch rather than technique. Esalen massage practitioners are expected to be able to tap into their clients’ cues, both physical and non-physical, so that he or she can identify where the client’s body is injured, or where there is an energy blockage without the client verbally expressing it.

Cranial-Sacral

Cranial-Sacral

The focus of cranial sacral massage is the central nervous system. The practitioner gently manipulates the head and neck of the client. This encourages the cerebrospinal fluid to flow more freely, and decreases structural strain on the CNS. Ultimately, cranial sacral Massage helps improve your ability to heal and allows your body to work at optimum levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your massage or bodywork session will take place in a warm, comfortable, quiet room. Soft music may be played to help you relax. You will lie on a massage table especially designed for your comfort.

Most massage and bodywork techniques are traditionally performed with the client unclothed; however, it is entirely up to you what you want to wear. You should undress to your level of comfort. You will be properly draped during the entire session.If you like Medical Massage can be done fully clothed if wearing loose clothing.

The practitioner will leave the room while you undress, relax onto the table, and cover yourself with a clean sheet or towel.

You will be properly draped at all times to keep you warm and comfortable. Only the area being worked on will be exposed.

A typical full-body session will include work on your back, glutes, arms, legs, feet, hands, head, neck, and shoulders.

A relaxing Swedish massage is often a baseline for clients. In a general Swedish massage, your session may start with broad, flowing strokes that will help calm your nervous system and relax exterior muscle tension. As your body becomes relaxed, pressure will gradually be increased to relax specific areas and relieve areas of muscular tension. Often, a light oil or lotion is used to allow your muscles to be massaged without causing excessive friction to the skin. The oil also helps hydrate your skin. You should communicate immediately if you feel any discomfort so that another approach may be taken. Always let the therapist know if anything makes you uncomfortable. Massage and bodywork are most effective when your body is not resisting.

There are numerous types of massage and bodywork; various techniques utilize different strokes, including basic rubbing strokes, rocking movement, posture and movement re-education, application of pressure to specific points, and more. We can discuss which methods may be most appropriate for you.

Prior to the massage, feel free to ask the practitioner any questions about the technique or the upcoming session. During the massage, make yourself comfortable. The practitioner will either gently move you or tell you what is needed throughout the session (such as lifting your arm). Many people just close their eyes and completely relax, communicating if/when they need more or less pressure, another blanket, or anything else relevant to the session. If you have any questions regarding the session or about the particular technique you are receiving, feel free to ask. And always communicate if anything makes you uncomfortable.
